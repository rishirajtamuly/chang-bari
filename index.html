About CHANG BARI

More than a farm. A living ecosystem.

A Homestead Inspired by Nature

CHANG BARI was created from a deep love for nature and a dream of building a place where biodiversity, sustainable farming and Assamese heritage exist together in harmony.

Our goal is not simply to grow crops. We are planting forests, creating habitats, restoring ecological balance and providing food for birds, butterflies, bees, squirrels and wildlife alongside our own family.

Every pond, every fruit tree, every timber tree and every corner of CHANG BARI has been planned with nature in mind.

Our Vision

We believe that a farm should be more than a place of production.

It should become a living ecosystem where native trees, fish ponds, orchards, livestock and wildlife flourish together.

We plant trees not only for ourselves, but also for hornbills, barbets, bulbuls, fruit bats, butterflies, squirrels and countless other creatures that depend upon healthy forests.

"Every Tree Has a Purpose. Every Life Has a Place."

Our Living Ecosystem

Every part of CHANG BARI works together to support a healthy environment.

🐟 Fishery

Healthy ponds that provide fish while creating habitat for frogs, dragonflies and aquatic biodiversity.

Sustainable pig farming integrated with the overall ecosystem of CHANG BARI.

🐔 Poultry

Free-ranging poultry raised in harmony with the surrounding landscape.

🦆 Duck Farming

Ducks naturally complement our ponds and contribute to a balanced farming system.

A diverse collection of native and traditional fruit trees providing food for both people and wildlife.

Supporting Biodiversity

CHANG BARI is being developed with the hope that birds, butterflies, bees, squirrels, fruit bats and many other forms of wildlife will find food and shelter here. One of our most treasured trees is the Cluster Fig (Ficus racemosa), a keystone species whose fruits are especially important for the Great Indian Hornbill and many other forest animals.
